Refund Policy

Transparency builds trust. This policy ensures fairness for all users.

1. Overview

This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued for BlockVault services, including subscriptions, AI credits, and enterprise plans.

By using BlockVault, you agree to this policy.

2. Nature of Service

BlockVault is a digital SaaS platform. Due to the nature of digital services and instant access:

  • Services are considered consumed upon activation
  • Data processing, AI validation, and blockchain transactions are irreversible

3. Subscription Refunds

❌ No Refund Policy (Standard)

All subscription payments are non-refundable. There are no refunds for:

  • Partial usage
  • Unused time
  • Plan downgrades

✅ Exceptions (Eligible for Refund)

Refunds may be issued ONLY if:

  • Payment was made by mistake (duplicate charge)
  • Service was not accessible due to platform failure
  • A billing error occurred on our side
This aligns with common SaaS practices where subscription fees are non-refundable once services are accessed.

4. AI Credits & Usage-Based Payments

  • AI validation credits are non-refundable once used
  • Unused credits:
    • May expire based on plan limitations
    • Are strictly non-transferable

5. Enterprise & Custom Plans

  • Refund terms are defined directly in the custom contract
  • These may include:
    • SLA-based credits
    • Partial refunds assessed on a case-by-case basis

6. Faulty or Non-Functional Service

If BlockVault services are:

  • Not working as described
  • Experiencing major technical failure

You may be eligible for Service credits or a Partial/Full refund (Subject to internal review).

Refund rights may apply if services are faulty or not delivered as promised.

7. Cancellation Policy

  • Users can cancel anytime directly from their portal
  • Access will continue until the end of the current billing cycle
  • No prorated refunds will be issued for early cancellation

8. Refund Process

To request an eligible refund, please email our billing department:

Email Billing billing@blockvault.io

Ensure you include your Account Email, Transaction ID, and the Reason for the request.

9. Processing Time

  • Review Phase: 2–5 business days
  • Refund Phase (if approved): 5–10 business days depending on your banking provider

10. Policy Updates

BlockVault reserves the right to modify this policy at any time to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements.
