A Technical Explainer for Educators & Evaluators.

BlockVault is not just a storage system. It is a dynamic, secure, and intelligent credential verification platform combining Blockchain Immutability, AI-Validation, and User-Controlled Access.

1. The Problem Space

Institutions

Institutions receive repeated verification requests for the same documents, wasting administrative time and resources.

Students

Students struggle to access their own documents after graduation, with no portable, verifiable credential system.

Recruiters

Recruiters face significant delays validating candidate authenticity, heavily slowing down hiring pipelines.

*Existing systems like DigiLocker and APAAR act primarily as passive storage and lack instant verification, revocable access, and support for post-academic credentials.

2. System Architecture

A centralized SaaS platform enabling decentralized verification. Achieving Web2 usability alongside Web3 trust guarantees.

Hybrid Consensus Mechanism

Proof of Authentication

Only verified AICTE-approved institutions can join and issue credentials. No unverified actors.

Proof of History

Validation via trusted external identifiers (APAAR IDs), creating continuous verifiable histories.

Proof of Authority

Institutions are validators. Every certificate is cryptographically signed by its issuing node.

3. User Journeys

Issuing Authorities

1
Registration & Verification: Institution registers; Root Authority validates and approves access to set up a private credential network workspace.
2
Student Enrollment: Institution adds students, generating unique Digital Credential Passports equipped with Passport IDs and QR codes.
3
AI-Powered Issuing: Institution uploads documents (Marksheets, LORs). AI Layer extracts metadata, flags anomalies, and checks timeline consistency instantly.
4
Blockchain Hash & Store: A SHA-256 hash is generated from content and pushed to Hyperledger Fabric. Transaction ID generated. Certificate linked to student's passport.
5
Explorer & Management: Institutions view all issues via the private Explorer Dashboard and retain the ability to instantly revoke fraudulent or erroneous credentials.

Lifelong Owners

During Academics

  • • Enrolled automatically, triggering Passport Creation.
  • • Logs into dashboard to view incoming certificates (Marksheets, NOCs).
  • • Views robust chronological Credential Timeline with Blockchain Proofs attached.
  • • Bundles certificates to create Secure Share Links / QRs for internship hunting.

Post Graduation

  • • Passive platform becomes Active. Passport remains portable forever.
  • • Uploads external certifications (AWS, Coursera, work XP).
  • • AI Layer validation: assigns AI Confidence Scores to non-institutional documents based on template matching.
  • • Selectively curates recruiter bundles. Revokes access post-hiring.

Regulators & Auditors

1
Elevated Dashboard: Registers with proof of regulatory role. Gains bird's-eye view of all registered institutions and credential activities.
2
Blockchain Audits: Full access to the blockchain transaction explorer to monitor exact timestamps, issuers, and modification patterns.
3
Deep Verification: Can request decryption access to view protected metadata. Cross-references data against external regulatory records.
4
Impact: Generates tamper-proof audit reports backed by blockchain logic. Systematically curtails credential forgery.
