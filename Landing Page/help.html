Getting Started

BlockVault is a blockchain-powered credential verification platform that allows:

  • Institutions to issue tamper-proof credentials
  • Students to own and share credentials
  • Recruiters to instantly verify authenticity
👤

For Students

  1. Log into your dashboard
  2. Open your Credential Passport
  3. View all issued certificates
  • Select certificates
  • Generate a QR code or secure link
  • Share with recruiter/institution

Yes — you can revoke any shared link anytime directly from your dashboard.

🏫

For Institutions

  1. Upload document (PDF, PNG, JPEG)
  2. AI validates metadata extraction
  3. Blockchain hash is automatically generated
  4. Credential is permanently recorded on the ledger

No — they are immutable once written to the blockchain. However, they can be revoked and reissued as a new block entry.

🏢

For Recruiters

Use the Verify portal to check credentials immediately. You can:

  • Scan the QR code provided by the candidate
  • Enter the Passport ID manually

You'll instantly see:

  • ✓ Verification status
  • ✓ Blockchain proof (SHA-256)
  • ✓ AI confidence score
🔐

Security & Trust

  • Blockchain ensures data cannot be altered by anyone, including server admins.
  • AI detects anomaly patterns and formatting fraud at the moment of issuance.
  • Every credential has a unique, globally-verifiable cryptographic hash.
⚙️

Common Issues

  • Contact your institution directly to ensure it has been fully processed.
  • Ensure you have registered with the exact matching email used by your institution.
  • Check if the candidate or institution has explicitly revoked the credential.
  • Ensure you are querying the correct Passport ID.
  • If you suspect fraud, use our Report Fraud portal.
