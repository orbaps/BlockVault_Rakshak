Report Fraud

Help us maintain a trusted ecosystem. If you suspect fraudulent credentials, misuse, or suspicious activity, report it immediately.

Confidential & Secure

🚨 What can be reported?

  • • Fake or altered certificates
  • • Suspicious credential activity
  • • Unauthorized access or sharing
  • • Misuse of institution accounts

📝 How to Report

1

Identify the Issue

Clearly describe what seems suspicious.

👉 Look for:
  • • Incorrect data on the credential card
  • • Unverified institution warnings
  • • Mismatched avatar/candidate details
2

Collect Evidence

Provide screenshots, QR links, Passport IDs, or Transaction IDs (if available). Collecting evidence is crucial for our AI + manual validation process.

🔐 What Happens Next?

  • 1. Case is logged into securely.
  • 2. AI and system analysis begins extracting metadata anomalies.
  • 3. Investigation team reviews the evidence manually.
  • 4. Action is enforced (e.g., permanent revocation) if fraud is confirmed.
Initial Review < 24 Hours
Full Investigation 2-5 Business Days
